GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF BUSINESS

1. Reservation/Order

A deposit of 20% of the sales price is required to order or reserve goods. The deposit must be paid within 48 hours; otherwise, the goods will be released for sale again. If the reservation or order is cancelled by the customer, we reserve the right to retain the 20% deposit as compensation.

2. Cancellation Policy / Returns

You can cancel your contract within 14 days by sending a written notice via email, stating your reasons. The cancellation period begins after receipt of your order or placement of the order. To meet the cancellation deadline, it is sufficient to send the cancellation notice before the deadline expires. The cancellation notice should be sent to the following address:

Raven Weapon AG
Sunnenbergstrasse 2
8633 Wolfhausen

The right of withdrawal applies only to goods available in the online shop. Goods specifically ordered at the customer's request are not subject to the right of withdrawal.

2.1 Consequences of the revocation

Exercising your right of withdrawal transforms the purchase agreement into a reversal transaction, whereby any payments received under the purchase agreement must be refunded. Received goods must be returned no later than 14 days after you declare your withdrawal. We will also refund any payments received within 14 days of you declaring your withdrawal. However, we may withhold reimbursement until we have received the goods back or you have provided proof that you have returned them, whichever is earlier.

Please note that we do not offer a right of withdrawal for the following products:

  • Returns of used items.
  • Items without or with damaged original packaging.
  • Transport damage (according to paragraph 2.2).

In the case of an unjustified return, this should also be noted briefly. You are responsible for the return shipping costs. We reserve the right to deduct compensation for any damage or excessive wear and tear from the refundable purchase price if the loss in value is due to handling of the goods beyond what is necessary to ascertain their nature, characteristics, and functioning.

Should a shipment be returned to us for the following reasons, the order will be automatically cancelled after notification to the customer:

  • The package was not picked up.
  • Recipient unknown.

In special cases, it is possible to resend the package; however, a flat fee of CHF 10.00 will be charged for this.

2.2 Damage or loss

Did your order arrive damaged? (Postal Service)

If your shipment arrives damaged, please take the damaged product, along with its outer packaging and packing material, immediately to your local post office and fill out a damage report. Request a copy and send it to us as a photo or scan to info@ravenweapon.ch so that you can claim your right to undamaged goods.

Damage that is not externally visible must be reported to the responsible post office in your municipality within 14 days of delivery.

Have you not received your order? (Post)

If the shipment does not arrive within 5 days of receiving the shipping confirmation, it is the customer's responsibility to notify us immediately.

We generally ship via Swiss Post or DPD. Shipping costs are calculated according to the current rates of the respective providers.
