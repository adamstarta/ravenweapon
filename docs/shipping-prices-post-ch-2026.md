# Post.ch Shipping Prices 2026

Official Swiss Post pricing for Raven Weapon AG shipping configuration.

## Domestic Switzerland (PostPac Priority)

Delivery: 1-2 business days within Switzerland and Liechtenstein.

| Weight | Post.ch Price |
|--------|---------------|
| Up to 2 kg | CHF 10.50 |
| Up to 10 kg | CHF 13.50 |
| Up to 30 kg | CHF 22.50 |

**Online discount:** CHF 1.50 off when creating labels via Post.ch online service or Post-App.

## International (PostPac International)

Delivery: Varies by destination (typically 4-10 business days).

| Weight | Post.ch Price |
|--------|---------------|
| Up to 2 kg | CHF 36.00 |
| Up to 5 kg | CHF 46.00 |
| Up to 10 kg | CHF 52.00 |
| Up to 15 kg | CHF 59.00 |
| Up to 20 kg | CHF 65.00 |
| Up to 30 kg | CHF 76.00 |

**Notes:**
- All prices in CHF, VAT-exempt for international
- Maximum parcel weight: 30 kg (some countries limited to 20 kg or 10 kg)
- Additional insurance (up to CHF 3,000): +CHF 15.00
- Oversized items: +CHF 15.00 surcharge

## Shop Configuration

### Swiss Customers (delivery address in Switzerland)
- **Standard:** Weight-based pricing per PostPac Priority rates
- **Express:** Weight-based pricing per PostPac Priority rates

### International Customers (delivery address outside Switzerland)
- **International:** Weight-based pricing per PostPac International rates
